## Quick-Reference: Posting Checklist (Do This Every Day)

1. **Post in at least 3–5 local Facebook "Buy Nothing / Community / Neighborhood" groups** (see group list tips below).
2. **Post on Nextdoor** in your service neighborhood.
3. **Reply to EVERY comment within 15 minutes** — speed wins the job.
4. **Always end with a direct CTA** (Call/Text number visible).
5. **Use 1–3 real before/after photos whenever possible** — posts with photos get 3× more engagement.
6. **Rotate post types** (story, deal, tip, testimonial, question) to avoid looking spammy.

## 📌 Posting Strategy — Maximize Calls & Close Rate

### Best Times to Post
| Platform | Best Days | Best Times |
|---|---|---|
| Facebook Groups | Mon, Wed, Fri | 7–9 AM or 6–8 PM |
| Nextdoor | Tue, Thu, Sat | 8–10 AM or 5–7 PM |
| Facebook Page | Daily | 8 AM & 6 PM |

### Top Facebook Group Types to Join & Post In
- **"[City] Buy Nothing" groups** — high engagement, neighbor trust
- **"[City] Neighborhood / Community" groups**
- **"[City] For Sale / Free Stuff" groups**
- **"Inland Empire Homeowners / Landlords" groups**
- **"Chino / Ontario / Rancho Cucamonga Community" groups**
- **"IE Real Estate Investors" groups** — investors always need cleanouts
- **"[City] Moms / Parents" groups** — they often manage home cleanouts and moves

### Keys to a High Close Rate
1. **Respond in under 15 minutes** — the first person who responds usually gets the job.
2. **Ask for a photo via text** — it shows professionalism and lets you quote fast.
3. **Give a firm price** — people hate "it depends." Give a range and then confirm on-site.
4. **Follow up** — if someone doesn't book, text them 24 hours later: *"Hey, just checking in — still have a spot open this week if you'd like to get that taken care of!"*
5. **Ask for Google reviews** after every completed job — even one review a week builds long-term lead flow.

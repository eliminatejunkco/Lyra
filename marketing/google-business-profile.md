## Part 2 — Google Local Services Ads (Pay-Per-Lead)

> **What it is:** Google's job-matching program for service businesses. You pay only when a verified customer contacts you through Google — not per click. The "Google Guaranteed" badge dramatically increases trust and calls.

### How It Works
- You set a weekly budget (suggest starting at **$100–$150/week**)
- Google shows your profile at the very top of search results with a green "Google Guaranteed" checkmark
- Customers click your listing → call or message you
- You pay only for valid leads (you can dispute bad leads for a refund)

### How to Sign Up
1. Go to [ads.google.com/local-services-ads](https://ads.google.com/local-services-ads)
2. Choose **Garbage Removal Service** category
3. Enter your service area
4. Complete the **Google Guarantee background check** (required — takes ~1–2 weeks)
5. Set your weekly budget and go live

### Required for Google Guarantee:
- [ ] Liability insurance (required — at least $1M general liability)
- [ ] Background check passed
- [ ] Business license (if required in your city)

### Expected Results
With a $100–$150/week budget in the Inland Empire, expect:
- **3–8 qualified leads per week** at an average cost of $15–$35 per lead
- Leads are people who actively searched for junk removal — very high intent
- Close rate is typically 50–70% once you respond fast

### Tips to Win Local Services Ads
1. **Answer every call** — unanswered leads are wasted money
2. **Set your hours accurately** — only show ads when you can answer
3. **Respond to messages within minutes** — Google's ranking algorithm rewards responsiveness
4. **Get reviews from Google LSA jobs** — more reviews = higher placement

## Part 1 — Customer Referral Program

### How It Works

Tell every happy customer:

> *"If you send anyone our way and they book a job, I'll give you [reward] as a thank-you."*

### Recommended Rewards

| Reward Option | Best For |
|---|---|
| **$25 cash/Venmo** | Everyone — simple and motivating |
| **$25 off their next job** | Customers likely to use you again (landlords, PMs) |
| **$10 Amazon gift card** | Casual homeowners |
| **Free single-item pickup** | Good for building loyalty |

### How to Talk About It

**After completing a job:**
> *"Hey [Name], if you know anyone — friends, family, neighbors, coworkers — who needs junk hauled, just send them my number. If they book a job, I'll send you $25 right away as a thank-you. I build my business on referrals."*

**Text to send after every job:**
```
Hey [Name], really appreciated working with you today! Quick thing — if anyone you know ever needs junk removal, just have them mention your name when they call. If they book, I'll send you $25 via Venmo as a thanks 🙏

— Sir Diego | Eliminate Junk Removal | (909) 373-5516
```

### Tracking Referrals
Keep a simple list in your Notes app:
- Who referred
- Who was referred
- Was the job booked?
- Reward sent? ✅

## Part 2 — Realtor & Real Estate Agent Partnerships

### Why Realtors Are Your Best Partners

- They list 10–30+ homes per year
- Every listing often needs a **cleanout before going on market**
- Move-out sellers often need a cleanout **before closing**
- They talk to hundreds of homeowners — one relationship = many jobs

### How to Find Realtors

1. **Search Zillow/Redfin/Realtor.com** for the most active agents in Chino, Ontario, Rancho Cucamonga, Chino Hills
2. **Search Google:** *"top realtor Chino CA"* → visit their website → find contact info
3. **Visit open houses** on weekends — introduce yourself in person
4. **Join local Facebook groups for real estate investors / agents**

### Realtor Outreach Script (Text or Email)

```
Hi [Name],

My name is Sir Diego — I own Eliminate Junk Removal, a junk hauling and cleanout service based in the Inland Empire.

I work with several realtors and help them get properties market-ready fast — full cleanouts, furniture removal, estate clearing. We typically turn jobs around same week, provide before/after photos, and offer consistent pricing.

If you ever need a reliable crew for a listing cleanout or move-out situation, I'd love to be your go-to. I'm happy to offer a preferred-partner rate for regular referrals.

Would a quick call this week work?

— Sir Diego | Eliminate Junk Removal
📞 (909) 373-5516
```

### Realtor Referral Deal to Offer

> *"For every job you refer that books, I'll send you $50 Venmo or a restaurant gift card — or I can give your clients a 10% discount, whichever you prefer."*

## Part 3 — Property Manager Partnerships

### Why Property Managers Are High-Value Partners

- They manage multiple rental units
- Tenant turnover = cleanout needed (2–6 times per year per unit)
- They often manage 10–100+ units = steady repeat business
- They want **reliability and fast turnaround** more than rock-bottom prices

### How to Find Property Managers

1. Search Google: *"property management company Inland Empire"*, *"property manager Chino CA"*
2. Look on **Yelp** and **Google Maps** for local PM companies
3. Search Facebook for *"Inland Empire Property Management"* and *"IE Landlords"* groups
4. Visit **Apartments.com / Zillow Rentals** and look for management company listings

### Property Manager Outreach Script

## Part 4 — Contractor & Handyman Partnerships

### Who to Target

- **General contractors** doing remodels (they generate construction debris)
- **Handymen** who get calls they can't handle (debris, heavy items)
- **Painters & flooring installers** who sometimes need old materials removed
- **Plumbers & HVAC techs** removing old appliances

### Where to Find Them

- Search Facebook groups: *"Inland Empire Contractors"*, *"IE Home Improvement"*
- Craigslist Services section — find local handymen and message them
- Nextdoor — look for contractor posts in your area
- Ask at Home Depot or Lowe's lumber/contractor desk

### Contractor Outreach Script

## Part 5 — Senior Care & Moving Company Partnerships

### Senior Care Providers

Assisted living facilities, senior move managers, and in-home care companies regularly help seniors downsize. They often need a hauling partner.

- Search: *"senior move manager Inland Empire"*, *"senior care Chino CA"*
- Introduce yourself as a **compassionate, patient cleanout service** that handles seniors with care

### Moving Companies

Moving companies often encounter clients who need junk hauled BEFORE or AFTER a move.

- Look up local movers on Google and Yelp
- Offer a mutual referral: *"You send me pre-move declutter jobs, I'll refer people who need movers to you."*
